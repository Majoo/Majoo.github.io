DataTjej Konferens
Branding, Public Relations, Web Design, Social Media

DataTjej Konferens is the largest conference for women studying Software and Computer Science related fields in the Nordic Area. I had the privilege of creating the brand of the conference 2015 including logo renewal, posters, a pamflet, stickers, patches and a redesign and reimplementation of the website. I was responsible for how we appeared in social media and for organizing photographers.
