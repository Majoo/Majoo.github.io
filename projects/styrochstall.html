Styr och Ställ
Usability, User Testing, Redesign

Styr-och-Ställ is a service for short-term leasing of bikes around town. We created a redesign of their digital and physical interface by performing usability tests of the original interface with users in a lab environment. The new design was based on the findings of the usability test, and after construction tested in the same way.

The biggest issue we found was that 90% of the testers thought it was a touchscreen, due to problematic placement of navigation buttons. In fact the card terminal buttons were used. Since we were not allowed to change the screen technology we instead added navigation buttons mapped to the screen.

The test subjects had trouble finding the desired functionality due to inconsistent layout and dead ends. By fixing these problems we could in the second usability test see a significant increase in effectiveness, efficiency and satisfaction. The project was performed in a team with five other design students.
