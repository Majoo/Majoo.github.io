knowme.earth
Mobile UI/UX, User Research, Master Thesis

Knowme.earth makes a mobile app and website through which you can log photos of species and get help identifying them, or be the identifier who helps others.

Before the mobile version was made, I studied the user groups and made prototypes. This included interviews, field observations, a workshop, paper sketches, mockups and evaluation. I did three iterations and came up with user goals, requirements and a design. I studied motivation in general and motivation in the crowdsourced species identification scene specifically, to match the design to the most common motivational factors. It was my master thesis in Interaction Design.
