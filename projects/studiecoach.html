StudieCoach
Visual Design, Web Design

A mobile application for new university students, helping them to stay both productive and happy! Chalmers University of Technology requested a mobile application to support students in their work, especially new students readjusting from high school. To explore needs, we interviewed student counsellors and students, surveyed students, researched competitors and consulted literature.

We worked in a Scrum-like fashion with sprints and a backlog. The team consisted of six people. I contributed to all areas including building the backend, designing the front-end, performing research and planning the design and implementation work. It was our Bachelor Thesis project and a lot of energy went into Android programming.
